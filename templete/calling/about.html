About Us

Empowering education through research

RIPITT Foundation is a leading educational research agency based in Bhubaneswar. We are dedicated to empowering education through cutting-edge research, innovative solutions, and impactful collaborations. Our mission is to drive positive change in the education sector by providing valuable insights and data-driven strategies. At RIPITT Foundation, we are committed to shaping the future of education through knowledge and expertise. Join us in our journey to transform the educational landscape.
